Hosted by the Department of Physics, Computer Science and Engineering at Christopher Newport University, Pizza My Mind gives you the chance to showcase career and internship opportunities to exceptional students majoring in related fields.

This initiative has grown into a major success with recruiters and students alike. Employers typically conduct a 10-to-15 minute presentation, providing time for questions and answers. To assist you, technical support is provided for MS PowerPoint, Web access, etc.

When:

Thursdays, 12:20-1:15 p.m., during fall and spring semesters

Where:

Luter Hall 121 ● Christopher Newport University
1 Avenue of the Arts ● Newport News, VA 23606

Typical Majors of Students:

Computer engineering, computer science, information science, information systems, electrical engineering, applied physics

Attendance:

Approximately 85-140 students

Seminar Registration Fee:

$250, which includes cost of pizza, drinks and paper/plastic ware
